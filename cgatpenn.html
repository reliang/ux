CG@Penn Website Revamp


Year: 2019
Duration: 2 months
Team Size: 2 (me and a developing partner)

Area: Web Design, Web Development
Tools: Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery
Type: Summer Internship

During summer 2019, I was given the task of redesigning the offical website for the Computer Graphics Department at UPenn. After talking to the head professor of the department, I learned that the old website is outdated and disorganized. Together, we identified several problems, such as pixelated logos, superfluous pages, low readability, and a website design that does not highlight the potential of Penn’s CG department.
